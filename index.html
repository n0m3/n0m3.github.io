Naomi Royall

Hi, I'm Naomi, a developer originally from England, currently living in Oakland, with a passion for User Experience design.

In 3 years as a front-end developer, I have developed the award-winning Global Campaign for Education, US website, the Play Healthy contest site run by MLB and Partnership for Drugfree Kids, and working prototypes for Hunters Alley.

I regularly use Grunt, Sass, Foundation & Git to build sites on Expression Engine, Craft & Statamic. I favor OOCSS, prefer templating in Twig, and am currently learning Slim & Laravel. You can read some of my tech writing here and here.

An appreciation of immersive, emotional design, affinity to detail, fascination with the human psyche, and longstanding interest in the art of storyboarding make UX the most appealing aspect of development. I enjoy collaborating closely with designers and strive to write perfomant and maintainable code.
